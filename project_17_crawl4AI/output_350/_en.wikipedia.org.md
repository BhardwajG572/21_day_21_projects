[![Ko Ping-chung, the tournament winner](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9c/Maldives_Open_2023_Final_-_Ko_Ping_Chung.jpg/250px-Maldives_Open_2023_Final_-_Ko_Ping_Chung.jpg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Maldives_Open_2023_Final_-_Ko_Ping_Chung.jpg "Ko Ping-chung, the tournament winner")
Ko Ping-chung, the tournament winner
The **[2019 WPA World Ten-ball Championship](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2019_WPA_World_Ten-ball_Championship "2019 WPA World Ten-ball Championship")** was a professional [pool](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pool_\(cue_sports\) "Pool \(cue sports\)") tournament for the discipline of [ten-ball](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ten-ball "Ten-ball") organised by the [World Pool-Billiard Association](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Pool_Association "World Pool Association") (WPA) and [CueSports International](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Billiard_Congress_of_America#CueSports_International "Billiard Congress of America"). It was the fifth [WPA World Ten-ball Championship](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WPA_World_Ten-ball_Championship "WPA World Ten-ball Championship"); the [previous championship](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_WPA_World_Ten-ball_Championship "2015 WPA World Ten-ball Championship") was held in 2015. After plans for an event in both 2016 and 2018 to be held in [Manila](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manila "Manila") fell through, a 2019 event at the [Rio All-Suite Hotel & Casino](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rio_Las_Vegas "Rio Las Vegas") in [Las Vegas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Las_Vegas "Las Vegas") as part of a three-year deal for the event to be played in the United States was agreed. The event was held concurrently with the [Billiard Congress of America](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Billiard_Congress_of_America "Billiard Congress of America")'s National Ten-ball event from July 22 to 26. The event was sponsored by cue manufacturer Predator Group. The competition featured 64 participants, selected according to world and continental pool rankings as well as qualifying events. [Ko Ping-chung](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ko_Ping-chung "Ko Ping-chung") _(pictured)_ , representing Taiwan, won the event, defeating German player [Joshua Filler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joshua_Filler "Joshua Filler") 10‚Äì7 in the final. [![Epinephelus marginatus](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a9/Mero_%28Epinephelus_marginatus%29%2C_Cabo_de_Palos%2C_Espa%C3%B1a%2C_2022-07-15%2C_DD_34.jpg/500px-Mero_%28Epinephelus_marginatus%29%2C_Cabo_de_Palos%2C_Espa%C3%B1a%2C_2022-07-15%2C_DD_34.jpg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Mero_\(Epinephelus_marginatus\),_Cabo_de_Palos,_Espa%C3%B1a,_2022-07-15,_DD_34.jpg "Epinephelus marginatus") |  _**[Epinephelus marginatus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Epinephelus_marginatus "Epinephelus marginatus")**_ is a species of fish in the [grouper](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grouper "Grouper") family, Epinephelidae. It is found in coastal waters, primarily at the edges of the Atlantic Ocean ‚Äì off western Africa and eastern South America ‚Äì and also in the Indian Ocean around South Africa, Madagascar and [R√©union](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/R%C3%A9union "R√©union"), and throughout the [Mediterranean](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mediterranean_Sea "Mediterranean Sea"). _Epinephelus marginatus_ is a very large, oval-bodied and large-headed fish with a wide mouth which has a protruding lower jaw. It is typically 90 cm in length but some individuals grow up to 150 cm. The head and upper body are coloured dark reddish brown or greyish, usually with yellowish gold countershading on the ventral surfaces, while the base colour is marked by a vertical series of irregular pale greenish yellow or silvery grey or whitish blotching. This _E. marginatus_ individual was photographed off [Cape Palos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cape_Palos "Cape Palos"), Spain.
